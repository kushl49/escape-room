Frequently Asked Questions

What is an escape room puzzle?

An escape room puzzle is a immersive, interactive game where participants work together to solve a series of puzzles, find clues, and accomplish tasks in order to "escape" from a themed room within a set time limit.

Can I play alone or do I need a team?

While it's possible to play alone, escape room puzzles are more enjoyable and manageable with a team of friends or family members. Teamwork and collaboration are key to solving the puzzles within the time limit.

What equipment or software do I need to play the escape room puzzles online?

All you need is a computer or tablet with an internet connection and a modern web browser. No additional software or downloads are required.

Can I pause the game and resume later?

Unfortunately, our escape room puzzles are designed to be completed in one session. Pausing and resuming the game later is not supported.
